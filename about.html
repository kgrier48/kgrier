ABOUT ME

After graduating with a BFA in Graphic Design, specializing in Intertactive Design I enlisted in the U.S. Navy as a Nuclear Engineer on Submarine duty. After realizing it wasn’t for me, I left the Navy to pursue the designer career.

Through many twists and turns I left my home state and got a job as a pre-press specialist. I worked long hours setting up designer files, imposing them on large 5 color metal plates and hand delivered the plates to a pressman.

Once I got an opportunity to work at a small agency; I took it immediately. Being on a very small design team I took on a lot of responsibilities. This included talking with developers, printers, and clients. I was fully immersed in the agency world.

Later I got the opportunity to make great work with another small agency in Kentucky. This was my start of doing full-stack development, as well as working with animators.

Realizing I needed someone to challenge my creative decision-making, I joined the largest agency in town, Cornett. I’ve enjoyed the struggle, the climb, and the constant challenge.
